Welcome to Your Business Website

This is a sample page demonstrating how the AIMeet chat widget can be embedded on any website. The widget provides AI-powered customer support and appointment booking capabilities.

Features:

  • 24/7 AI-powered customer support
  • Appointment booking with calendar selection
  • Email confirmations for appointments
  • Smart availability checking to prevent double-bookings
  • Professional, responsive design

How to Embed on Your Website:

Simply add this one line of code before the closing </body> tag:

<script
  src="http://localhost:3000/widget.js"
  data-widget-key="YOUR_WIDGET_KEY"
></script>
üìù Note: Replace YOUR_WIDGET_KEY with your actual widget key (e.g., 1919777b-b7ad-496f-b919-194402d044ff)
